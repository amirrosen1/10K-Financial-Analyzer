Content from iTunes Radio, Beats Music™ and other media services, including Netflix, YouTube, Flickr, Hulu Plus, Vevo, MLB, NBA and NHL is available on Apple TV. Apple TV allows streaming iTunes content from Macs and Windows personal computers through Home Share and through AirPlay® from compatible Mac and iOS devices. Compatible Mac and iOS devices can also mirror their device screens as well as stream and play games on Apple TV.
